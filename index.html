About the Course

Course Code: 24EMAB233

Probability & Statistics focuses on data analysis, probability models, random variables, statistical inference, and hypothesis testing. This course builds strong analytical and problem-solving skills.

Syllabus & Topics

Unit 1: Description of Data

Types of data, graphical representation, mean, median, mode, variance, standard deviation, skewness.

Unit 2: Probability

Basic probability rules, conditional probability, Bayes’ theorem, real-world applications.

Unit 3: Random Variables & Distributions

Discrete and continuous random variables, Binomial, Poisson and Normal distributions.

Unit 4: Statistical Inference

Sampling methods, estimation, confidence intervals, hypothesis testing.

Laboratory Work

Data visualization, probability simulation, distribution analysis, hypothesis testing using Python.
